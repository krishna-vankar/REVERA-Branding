# REVERA-Branding
Submission Note – REVERA Branding Set
Company Base & Motto  
REVERA is an event management brand built on the motto “Turning Vision Into Reality.” The core idea is to focus on client needs, transforming their vision into memorable experiences through creativity, precision, and flawless execution.

Logo  
The logo features a stylized “R” with a figure reaching for a star, symbolizing ambition, transformation, and success. It represents REVERA’s promise to elevate client visions into reality.

Meaning: The star signifies achievement and dreams, while the upward motion reflects growth and celebration.

Color Palette  
Dark Blue, White, and Gold were chosen deliberately:

Dark Blue: Trust, professionalism, and stability.

White: Clarity, simplicity, and honesty.

Gold: Luxury, success, and memorable highlights.
Together, they create a premium and elegant brand identity.

Poster/Flyer  
The poster and flyer communicate REVERA’s services with bold headlines and elegant visuals. They highlight the brand’s professionalism and invite clients to experience unforgettable events.

Business Card  
The business card design is clean and professional, using the logo, tagline, and essential contact details. It reinforces REVERA’s identity in networking and client interactions.

Infographic  
The infographic explains REVERA’s process in three clear steps:

Understanding Your Vision – Listening to client needs.

Tailored Planning – Designing personalized strategies.

Memorable Execution – Delivering flawless, unforgettable events.
This shows the client‑centric approach and makes the brand’s workflow easy to understand.
